Rule #1

🖊️

Wrong Ink Color
(Blue vs Black)

❌ Mistake: Blue ya kisi aur colored ink se sign karna.
✍️ Correct Way: Portals (SSC, UPSC etc.) strictly black ink pen require karte hain taaki machine scans bilkul clean rahein.

Always use BLACK INK pen 🖊️

Rule #2

🗒️

Ruled Paper &
Gray Background

❌ Mistake: Line wale paper (ruled copy) par sign karna, ya low light me phone scan lena.
✍️ Correct Way: Plain white paper par sign karein aur shadows se bachein. Scanner background plain white hi mangte hain.

Plain white background is mandatory!

Rule #3

📏

Strict KB Size Limits
(10KB – 20KB)

❌ Mistake: 10KB se choti quality-less image upload karna, ya 20KB se badi original file upload karna.
UPSC/SSC portals exact 10KB–20KB signature file size require karte hain.

Wrong Size = Direct Rejection ❌
